==============
BroadcastEvent
==============

The **BroadcastEvent** intervention class immediately broadcasts the event trigger you specify. This campaign
event is typically used with other classes that monitor for a broadcast event, such as
:doc:`parameter-campaign-node-nodelevelhealthtriggerediv` or :doc:`parameter-campaign-event-communityhealthworkereventcoordinator`.
